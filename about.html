Asendant Global Enterprise, Inc. (AGE) is a veteran-owned strategic consulting and technology solutions firm incorporated in Delaware and headquartered in Aberdeen, Maryland.

AGE was founded on the conviction that government agencies and private enterprises deserve access to executive-caliber leadership and enterprise-grade expertise — not generalist advice, but mission-aligned counsel from leaders who have operated at the highest levels.

We operate at the intersection of organizational strategy, technology innovation, and mission-driven execution — helping clients solve today's challenges while building the institutional capacity to lead tomorrow.
